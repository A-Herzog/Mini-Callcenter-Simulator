On this statistics page, information on the waiting times of the clients is displayed. A distinction is made between the waiting times of successful clients and the waiting times of the waiting cancelers (i.e. the cancelation times). In both cases average values, standard deviations and coefficients of variations are displayed.

Service level

In addition, the service level is output at the 20 second threshold. This value indicates the proportion of clients who had to wait a maximum of 20 seconds.

Since the service level is closely related to the quantiles, the quantiles of the waiting times are also output in this section.
