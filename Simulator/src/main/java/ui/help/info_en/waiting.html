Waiting room and waiting time tolerance

If the size of the waiting room is limited, clients arriving while all waiting places are occupied will be rejected. Clients who are already being served do not occupy any space in the queue, i.e. a waiting room limitation only applies to waiting clients.

The waiting time tolerance can be used to control whether clients are willing to wait as long as needed or whether they will give up waiting at some point. The waiting time tolerance is not a constant value for all clients, but is determined individually for each client according to the set distribution.

Recording the autocorrelation of waiting times slows down the simulation, but allows to see how strong the influence of one client's waiting time is on the waiting times of the following clients.
