Arrivals

If the clients arrive individually (which is the normal case in a call center), the value in the field clients per arrival can be left at 1. If several clients arrive at the same time, a higher value can be entered here.

The distribution of the inter-arrival times determines the intervals between the arrival of the clients. In addition to the average distance between arrivals, the distribution also controls the variation of arrivals. If the clients arrive independently, the exponential distribution is the right choice due to its memorylessness.
