Model

This page shows the general design of the simulation model:

  • Clients arrive and line up in the queue - or are rejected if there is no more room in the queue.
  • Waiting clients may give up waiting if their personal waiting time tolerance is exceeded.
  • Clients who leave the queue without having been served may try again later.
  • If an agent becomes free, the service of the next waiting client is started.
  • After serving a client, an agent may go into an additional post-processing period before reporting as available again.
  • Clients will leave the system after being served or will be referred back to the queue if their request has not been completely clarified.

There are no settings available on this dialog page.
